Who we are

Through advocacy, resources, and unwavering solidarity, we illuminate the path forward, empowering each mother to embrace her unique role with courage.

Life of An Autistic Mum (LAMCF) was established in January 2023. Its main purpose is to enable and empower parents and caregivers of children and young people with special needs, particularly those children and young people who are not in education, training, or employment, so that they can learn skills and trades. We are committed to providing knowledge, advocating, and spreading awareness worldwide about autism and other neurological disorders affecting children and adults in our communities. As the founder of LAMCF and a mum myself, I believe that whenever a child is diagnosed with special needs, the parents will be confused and in denial. LAMCF is advocating for and reaching out to parents/caregivers to aid the acceptance process. Parents/caregivers should not be left alone and lost in the journey of caring for children with special needs.

What we do

We believe in the power of community and collaboration, working hand in hand with individuals, families, educators, and professionals to create a more inclusive and supportive environment for all

Spreading Awareness, Igniting Change

At the Life of an Autistic Mum Foundation, we are passionate about fostering understanding, compassion, and acceptance for autistic mothers and their families. Join us in our mission to spread awareness about autism spectrum disorder (ASD) and the unique experiences of mothers navigating this journey. Through education, advocacy, and community outreach, we aim to break down stereotypes, challenge stigmas, and promote inclusivity. Together, let's shine a light on the diverse strengths and perspectives within the autism community, empowering every mother to embrace her journey with pride and dignity. Together, we can ignite change and create a world where every autistic mother is seen, heard, and valued."

Our Volunteer Family Make a Difference

Are you passionate about supporting autistic mothers and their families? Consider becoming a part of our volunteer family at the Life of an Autistic Mum Foundation. Your dedication and enthusiasm can make a profound impact on the lives of those we serve.
